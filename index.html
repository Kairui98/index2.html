Jurong Lake Gardens is Singapore's new national gardens in the heartlands.
It is a people's garden, where spaces will be landscaped and created for families and the community to come together 😍

A little about Jurong Lake Gardens...
This 90-hectare Gardens is now opened from end-April 2019!

    The Gardens comprises of
  • Lakeside Garden (formerly Jurong Lake Gardens west)
  • Chinese and Japanese Gardens (formerly Jurong Lake Gardens Central)
  • Garden Promenade (formerly Jurong Lake Gardens East)

Construction for Chinese Garden, Japanese Garden and Garden Promenade will be completed from 2021 onwards.

Attractions

Clusia Cove

Is the weather too much for you to handle? Take a dip with your loved ones!
Clusia Cove is a three-hectare water playground where children can experience water movements
These inlcude mimic tidal patterns, surface ripples and directional currents similar to those at coastal shores.
Talk about a fun education!

Grasslands

Out of interesting locations for your next instagram post? Fear no more!
Dominated by tall grasses, Grasslands are an important habitat for many small bird species that feed on grass seeds and seek shelter in the grass.
Grasslands also house the Lone trees that is sculptured and made from recycled iron.
PS: Be sure to tag us in your pictures!

PAssion WaVe @ Jurong Lake Gardens

Feeling the need to address the adrenaline junkie in you? We got you covered!
PAssion WaVe @ Jurong Lake Gardens is the first waterfront facility in the west to provide residents with a myriad of water activities and lifestyle programs.
These range from paddle boating and kayaking to arts, food and beverage and health & wellness programs.
What are you waiting for? Come on down today!
